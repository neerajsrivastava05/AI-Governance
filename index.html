12.2 — Severity Reference
P1 CRITICALActive harm, data breach, regulator involvement, business-stopping
Response: 1 hour · Escalate to: CEO, CISO, Legal, Board (if required)
P2 HIGHMajor bias, significant performance degradation, regulatory breach risk
Response: 4 hours · Escalate to: CTO, CRO, AI Governance Lead, Legal
P3 MEDIUMModerate model drift, isolated decision errors, minor data quality issues
Response: 24 hours · Escalate to: AI Technical Review Board
P4 LOWMinor anomalies, low-impact issues, near-miss events
Response: 72 hours · Notify: AI Governance Lead (FYI)
